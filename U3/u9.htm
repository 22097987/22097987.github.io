Spreadsheets

This page is where I'll talk to you about Unit 9, Spreadsheets

Spreadsheets are used to sort data out, such as peoples names, where they live, putting them in alphabetical order, etc.

Spreadsheets can also be used to make tables and sort out difficult math sums.

Using spreadsheets allows for a variety of scenarios to be explored quickly so students can get a better intuitive sense of how a model works or what a data series shows.

Spreadsheets can also allow you to have different charts. (e.g. Pie, Line, Bar, etc)
