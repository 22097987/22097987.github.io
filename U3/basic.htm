Basic Skills

This page is where I'll talk to you about my basic skills.

Creating a personal progression plan can help me to prepare for the future and provide direction towards achieving your personal and professional goals and support personal growth and wellbeing.

For a progression plan to be useful to me, it should reflect where I am today and where I want to be in the future.

It should also help me to identify short-, mid- and long-term tasks to aid my progression.
