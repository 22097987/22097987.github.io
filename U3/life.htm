Life Skills

This page is where I'll talk to you about my life skills.

We all face different choices when we finish full-time education.

Some people may carry on learning in a further or higher education college or be accepted into university.

I might go onto an apprenticeship programme to work and study at the same time.

The appeal of working for myself may interest some people or part-time work may be more appropriate in meeting personal needs.

Whatever choice I make, I will anticipate receiving money for the work I do or support for my studies.

The amount of money I receive will vary depending on what I do but there is also support for those receiving a low income.

Being financially aware will help with my personal growth and wellbeing.
